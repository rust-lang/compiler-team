2019-03-12: Triage Meeting

Written by: @davidtwco

This week, the members of the async-await working group updated the group on their individual progress on assigned issues from previous meetings. Then, the group triaged all new issues from the week, looking at #59087 and #59001.

In summary, at the end of this meeting, the group is working on:

  • @gilescope is on #51751
  • @davidtwco is on #55809
  • @tmandry is continuing #52924
  • @cramertj is continuing on a fix for all of the lifetime-in-async-fn-declaration issues
  • @nikomatsakis is having a relaxing week
